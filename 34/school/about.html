About Us

ANNAI THERASA N&P-KEELAKURUCHI was established in 2003 and it is managed by the Pvt. Unaided. It is located in Rural area. It is located in MADUKKUR block of THANJAVUR district of Tamil Nadu. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 5. The school is Co-educational and it have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. English is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April.
